Prevention Tips

Indoor Tips

  • Use HEPA air purifiers
  • Keep windows closed during peak pollen times
  • Maintain indoor humidity between 30-50%
  • Regular vacuum with HEPA filter
  • Wash bedding weekly in hot water
  • Use allergen-proof bed covers

Outdoor Tips

  • Monitor local pollen forecasts
  • Wear sunglasses and hat outdoors
  • Change clothes after outdoor activities
  • Avoid going out during peak pollen hours
  • Use air conditioning in cars
  • Take allergy medication before symptoms start
